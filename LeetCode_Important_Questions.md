## Tips for Maximum Efficiency

### Time Management
- **Easy**: 15-20 minutes max
- **Medium**: 30-45 minutes max
- **Hard**: 45-60 minutes max

### When Stuck
1. Read problem 3 times carefully
2. Work through example manually
3. Think of brute force first
4. Look for patterns you know
5. After 30 min, check hints
6. After 45 min, check solution
7. Understand solution fully
8. Code it yourself without looking
9. Review after 1 day, 1 week, 1 month

### Study Strategy
1. **First Pass**: Understand problem + pattern
2. **Second Pass**: Implement solution
3. **Third Pass**: Optimize (time/space)
4. **Fourth Pass**: Explain to someone
5. **Spaced Repetition**: Review periodically

### Pattern Recognition
When you see a problem, ask:
- Can I use Two Pointers?
- Is this a Sliding Window?
- Do I need BFS or DFS?
- Can DP solve this?
- Is there a greedy approach?
- Should I use a specific data structure?
